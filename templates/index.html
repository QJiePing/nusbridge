What is NUS Bridge?

Have you ever had uncertainity in the quest for your hopes while you're still in university?

While keeping up with technical knowledge is important, we believe that who you know are the most important assets for your personal well-being or professional success. We also believe that knowing your flaws is also a strength because they can work against your goals when you interact with people.

This is all about you and your goals. In an academic environment, there may be people, other than your friends, who think like you or even better than you too.

So, we thought about an experiment. We created NUS Bridge to discover interesting people and experiences from the crowd relevant to your aspiration.

Indeed, this is just a beginning and your contribution to the crowd helps.
