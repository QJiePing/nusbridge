What is NUS Bridge?

Your connections are the most important assets for your personal well-being and professional success. It can be challenging to understand the value of your connections in an academic setting. Your social capital is present but it is either overlooked or not obvious.

Our mission is to make finding relevant people and experiences as easy as possible for your goals.
